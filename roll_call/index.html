Roll Call

Roller skating in San Diego
Rinks
Ringer's Roller Rink
Outdoor
Encanto · 6060 Federal Blvd
💰 $18 (skates included)
📅 7 days a week
⏱️ 3-hour sessions
San Diego's premier outdoor roller rink with a banked track and flat track. Hosts roller derby, '90s nights, and 21+ sessions with drinks. Art installations and selfie stations.
🌐 ringersrollerrink.com 📍 Map
Skateworld San Diego
Indoor
Linda Vista · 6907 Linda Vista Rd
💰 $15 + $7 skate rental
📅 Weekends + select weeknights
SD's only dedicated indoor rink — open since 1975. Classic circular wood floor with disco ball and LED light show. Family skate nights, adult nights with DJs (Tue/Thu), and youth roller derby.
🌐 skateworld.com 📍 Map
Free Outdoor Spots
Mission Beach Boardwalk
Free
Mission Beach / Pacific Beach
📏 3.5 miles
🌊 Beachside
San Diego's most iconic skating path — flat concrete boardwalk right alongside the beach with restaurants, shops, and people-watching the whole way.
📍 Map
The Embarcadero
Free
Downtown · 200 Marina Park Way
📏 8 miles roundtrip
⛵ Waterfront views
Long, smooth bayfront path past the USS Midway, Star of India, and the Unconditional Surrender statue. Flat and easy the whole way.
📍 Map
Liberty Station
Free
Point Loma · 2875 Dewey Rd
📏 1-mile paved path
🏛️ Historic Naval base
Smooth concrete through a beautifully restored former Naval Training Center. Community skating groups sometimes hold free lessons here.
📍 Map
Coastal Rail Trail
Free
Cardiff to Encinitas · S Coast Hwy 101
📏 3 miles out-and-back
🌿 Flat, fresh concrete
One of the smoothest stretches for skating in San Diego — newer flat concrete with a scenic coastal vibe, part of a planned 44-mile trail.
📍 Map
Silver Strand Bikeway
Free
Coronado to Imperial Beach
📏 ~9 miles one way
🏖️ Ocean on one side, bay on the other
A long, flat dedicated path along the Silver Strand peninsula with views of both the Pacific and San Diego Bay. One of the most scenic skating routes in the county.
📍 Map
Sunset View Park
Free
Chula Vista · 1390 S Greensview Dr
⏰ 7–10pm daily
🏒 Roller hockey rink
Outdoor roller hockey rink open to the public evenings when games aren't scheduled. Community meetups and skating events happen here regularly.
